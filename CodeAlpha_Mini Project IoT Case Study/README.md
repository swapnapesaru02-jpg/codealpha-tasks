Future of IoT in Smart Cities

 Project Overview

This project explains the Future of Internet of Things (IoT) in Smart Cities and how IoT technology helps improve urban living through automation, connectivity, and intelligent systems.

Smart cities use IoT devices and sensors to collect real-time data for better management of transportation, energy, healthcare, security, and public services.


---

 Objective

To study and understand:

The role of IoT in smart city development

How connected devices improve city infrastructure

Future applications of IoT technologies in urban environments



---

 What is IoT?

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to a network of connected devices that communicate and exchange data through the internet without human intervention.

Examples include:

Smart traffic lights

Smart parking systems

Smart energy meters

Smart healthcare monitoring



---
 Applications in Smart Cities

Smart Traffic Management

Waste Management Systems

Smart Street Lighting

Smart Water Monitoring

Smart Healthcare Systems

Intelligent Security & Surveillance

Energy-efficient Buildings



---

 How IoT Works in Smart Cities

1. Sensors collect real-time data from the environment.


2. Data is transmitted through the internet or cloud platforms.


3. The system analyzes the data using AI and automation.


4. Smart actions are performed automatically for better efficiency.

 Benefits

 Conclusion

IoT is transforming traditional cities into smart cities by improving efficiency, sustainability, and connectivity. In the future, IoT will play a major role in creating safer, smarter, and eco-friendly urban environments.
